# Cafe-Business-Performance-
☕ Cafe Business Performance Dashboard
A Power BI dashboard built to track revenue, profit, costs, and monthly trends for a cafe business.

What is this project?
A cafe had 3,200 orders over one year but the owner had no clear picture of:

Which items were making the most profit
Where costs were going
Which months were slow
Whether business growth was healthy

I built this dashboard to answer all these questions in one single screen.

Tools Used

Power BI — Dashboard and visualization
DAX — Measures for Revenue, Profit, AOV, Growth
Power Query — Date format conversion, data type check
Excel — Initial data exploration (null check, unique values, data types)


Dataset

3,200 orders — 15 columns
Order ID, Date, Month, Category, Item Name, Quantity, Unit Price, Total Amount, Food Cost, Labour Cost, Profit, Delivery Partner, Payment Mode


Business Problem
The cafe owner had no visibility into:

Which menu item makes the most profit
Where to cut costs
Which month will be slow
Is revenue growth healthy or not

5 Key Insights
Insight 1 — Revenue up but spending down:
Revenue grew 9.97% but AOV dropped -0.27% — more customers came but each spent less per visit → upselling needed
Insight 2 — Most profitable item:
Corn & Mayo Club made the highest profit ($1,336) — not the most popular item but the most profitable → promote it more
Insight 3 — Food cost too high:
Food Cost ($31K) was 2.5x higher than Labour Cost ($12K) → food procurement is biggest cost saving opportunity
Insight 4 — Seasonal gap:
November best month ($3,900) vs September worst ($2,566) → $1,334 gap → plan promotions for September in advance
Insight 5 — Digital payments dominate:
75.20% revenue from digital payments (UPI/Online/Card) vs 24.8% cash → business is digital-first
